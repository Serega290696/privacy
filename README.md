# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how **Beltser Serhii** ("we", "our", or "us") handles information in the **Horizons: Life Intent** mobile application.

## 1. Privacy Philosophy: Offline-First
**Horizons: Life Intent** is designed with a strict "privacy-by-default" approach. **We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers.** Your information remains entirely under your control on your mobile device.

## 2. Data Collection and Usage
All data generated within the app is stored strictly in the application's local database.

* **Local Storage:** Your goals, intentions, and time-tracking data are stored locally using on-device storage (SQLite/Room).
* **Microphone Access:** The app requests microphone access to enable voice-based interactions and notes. All audio processing is performed locally on the device. No audio files, recordings, or voice metadata are ever transmitted to the cloud or shared with third parties.

## 3. No Third-Party Services
We do not integrate any third-party analytics (such as Firebase Analytics), crash reporting tools (such as Crashlytics), or advertising SDKs. No data is shared with any third party, including the developer.

## 4. Data Retention and Deletion
Since all data is stored locally, it remains on your device as long as the app is installed. You can delete all your data at any time by:
* Clearing the app cache/data in your Android system settings.
* Uninstalling the application.

## 5. Compliance with GDPR (RODO)
As we are based in **Poland (EU)**, we adhere to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Because we do not collect or store your data on our servers, we cannot access, provide, or delete your data on your behalf. You exercise your data rights directly through the application management on your device.

## 6. Children’s Privacy
Our application does not knowingly collect any information from children, as no data collection occurs. The app is safe for all users who require a private goal-tracking tool.
